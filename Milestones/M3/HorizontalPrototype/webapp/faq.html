Frequently Asked Questions


How do I use FunBill?

FunBill is a web application for splitting expenses with your friends. It lets you and your friends add various transactions and keep track of who owes who. You can add friends and create groups with links or QR codes. Here's a quick overview of how it works.


First, sign up for an account!


Next, add a transaction. Here you can specify who you'd like to add to the bill, and how much their share of the bill will be. Once the transaction is created, if you're already friends with a person that owes you money, they'll be notified on their account. If you're planning to split with someone who hasn't signed up for FunBill, they can pay you back via link or QR code.


Once you've received a payment, you'll be able to view the receipt on your transaction logs. If a friend needs a reminder, you can do that within the website by clicking the reminder button next to the transaction details.


If you plan on having reoccurring expenses with certain people, consider creating a group.


Those are the basics, and should be enough to get you started. If you have more questions, check out the rest of our FAQ page!



How do I invite a friend?

Once you've logged into your account, at the bottom of the Dashboard menu you'll find a section that allows you to add a friend with their email. Type their email into the box and hit Invite.


If you've added the person to a transaction before, you can search for their name in the searchbar or previous transactions menu. Clicking on their name will give you an option to add them as a friend.


Once they've accepted the request, you can find their name under Friends, located on your Dashboard side panel.



How do I create a group?

Once you've logged into your account, the second row of of the Dashboard should display all your current groups. Click on the Groups header to open up a page listing all of your current groups. At the right corner of the page is a button to Create a Group. You can directly add friends to the group, or copy the invite link to be send to whomever.


Once the group has been created, you can view the group under Groups, located on your Dashboard.



What does FunBill have to offer?

  • Split bills with family, friends, colleagues, roommates, and more!
  • Calculate a simplified version of your debt, so there's no need to juggle multiple different transactions.
  • Create a budget plan for your next trip or vacation! Keep track of what you have left, including bill split with friends.
